What is the story behind OUI?

In 2023, Marcel Baron, founded the café OUI with a lifelong journey of bringing fine coffee flavours and desserts designated for all ages.
He set the mission of “bringing high-quality and affordable products to everyone around the world”. Took a long time through thick and thin, to begin the production of our core ingredients independently.
